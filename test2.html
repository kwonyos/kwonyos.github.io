3 / 3

Mtion of joint 2 and 3 in decoupling mechanism
  • In order to solve a motion-coupling problem in TDM, two identical planetary gear trains are combined into a dual-planetary gear train.
  • By attaching a dual-planetary gear train, the robot system can accomplish three-degree of freedom without motion-decoupling.
  • As a result, Joint 2 and Joint 3 are independently driven by Motor 2 (purple) and Motor 3 (green), respectively.
