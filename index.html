Yongseok Kwon

I am a robot researcher at the Republic of Korea Army Headquarters. Before joining the army, I worked in ROAHM Lab advised by Ram Vasudevan at the University of Michigan. Also, I received my BS(‘20) at UNIST, and MS(‘22) at the University of Michigan.

My research interests lie in safe reinforcement learning, task and motion planning, and neural verification. My research goal is to create reliable deep learning-based algorithms to deploy autonomous robots closer to our daily lives.
