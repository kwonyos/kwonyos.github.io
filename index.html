Bio

I am a first-year master student in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. My research interest lies in robotics, control theory, and machine learning.

My hometown is Gyeongju, Republic of Korea. Before enrolling at UMich, I received a Bachelor of Science degree at Ulsan National Institute of Science and Technology (UNIST) in 2020.
