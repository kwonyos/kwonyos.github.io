Bio

I am a robot researcher at Republic of Korea Army Headquaters. My research interest lies in safe reinforcement learning, task and motion planning, and neural verification.

My hometown is Gyeongju, Republic of Korea, where I was born and raised. Before joining the army, I received a Bachelor degree at Ulsan National Institute of Science and Technology (UNIST) in 2020, and a Master degree at the University of Michigan in 2022.
